Rules

Pig is a simple two player dice game which in its basic form is playable with just a single die. You win by being the first player to score 100 or more points.

So how do you play Pig?

Choose a player to go first. That player rolls a dice and scores as many points as the total shown on the Current score board providing the die doesnâ€™t roll a 1. The player may continue rolling and accumulating points (but risk rolling a 1) or end his turn by selecting Hold.

If the player rolls a 1 his turn is over, he loses all points accumulated in that turn, and passes the die to the next player.
If a player rolls two 6's consecutively then he loses all his total points and it resets to 0.

To hold onto the point accumulated in a turn, the player must select Hold and all the points will be added to his total score.

Play passes from player to player until a winner is determined.

How do you win?

The first player to accumulate 100 or more points wins the game.
